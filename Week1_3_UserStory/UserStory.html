Dale Carnegie

1888 - 1955

When dealing with people, let us remember we are not dealing with creatures of logic. We are dealing with creatures of emotion.

Dale Carnegie was the author of How to Win Friends and Influence People, one of the best-selling self-help books of all time. Other notable books are How to Stop Worrying and Start Living (1948), Lincoln the Unknown (1932) and others.
